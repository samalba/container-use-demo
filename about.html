About Sam Alba

Co-founder at Dagger, passionate about making complex software workflows more efficient

Who I Am

I'm Sam Alba, Co-founder and vision steward at Dagger, where since 2019 I've been working to revolutionize how developers approach complex software workflows. My journey in technology has taken me through the exciting worlds of containerization, open-source development, and AI-driven software tools.

Based in San Francisco, California, I'm passionate about building tools that empower developers and make software development more efficient and enjoyable. As a Y Combinator alumnus (W19 batch), I've had the privilege of working alongside some of the most innovative minds in technology.
